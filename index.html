Merge, grow, and bring the land back to life

Fieldcrest: Harvest Lines is a a chill puzzle game where you merge stuff and actually watch the farm come alive bit by bit. Start with scattered seeds, simple tools, and quiet empty plots. Combine crops, unlock new harvests, restore forgotten corners of the farm, and see empty plots turn into this amazing, bustling farm that's totally yours.

A quiet field with room to grow

You know that feeling when you take a patch of nothing and slowly turn it into something amazing? That's what this game's all about. That feeling sits at the heart of Fieldcrest: Harvest Lines. The game begins simply: a few seeds, a little space, and the promise that the field can become much more than it is now.

As you merge and rebuild, the farm begins to answer back. New rows appear. Fresh plots open. Harvests become richer. What once felt small and unfinished starts to feel cared for, productive, and alive. There's no rush here - it's more about that steady progress that just keeps you hooked because you can actually see everything changing.
